Chapter 2
Motivation
Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication. Leonardo da Vinci
The primary objective of this project is to design and develop a function generator
tailored to the specic needs of students enrolled in the ELP101 Basic Electronics Labora-
tory. Recognizing that these students are at the beginning of their journey in electronics,
we aim to create a device that is intuitive, cost-eective, and focused on fundamental
functionality.
While commercial function generators are readily available, they often include features
and complexities that can be intimidating for beginners. Our function generator will
address this by oering essential waveform generation capabilitiessuch as sine, square,
and triangular waveswhile maintaining simplicity and reliability suitable for a class-
room environment.
Beyond supporting standard laboratory experiments, this device will serve as a platform
for hands-on learning and exploration, encouraging students to experiment and innovate
beyond the curriculum. By bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practi-
cal application, this project aims to foster a deeper understanding of electronics, inspire
creativity, and empower students to condently engage in electronic design.
Our ultimate goal is to enhance the ELP101 lab experience, making it more
engaging and eective, while cultivating a passion for electronics among rst-
year students.
